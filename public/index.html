Introducing Ejo Labs

Ejo Labs builds production-ready AI systems that speak Kinyarwanda and understand Rwanda. We combine large language models, computer vision, and agentic workflows with deep local context to deliver sovereign solutions.
  • Local Expertise: Deep understanding of Rwanda's language, culture, and business environment.
