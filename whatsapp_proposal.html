The WhatsApp Engagement Opportunity

Over 90% of active mobile internet users in Rwanda use WhatsApp as their primary communication channel. Yet, large enterprises still force customers to rely on expensive call centers or complex web portals.

The Multi-Language Gap

Standard automated support systems fail to scale across multiple customer languages. They sound robotic in English and French, or fail to support local African languages like Kinyarwanda and Swahili, leaving customers alienated.

Multilingual Advantage

Our models support all major languages seamlessly. By deploying the WhatsApp Chatbot Webhook, users can text or send voice notes in Kinyarwanda, English, French, or Swahili, and receive fluent, culturally aware responses.

System Integration Architecture

1. User

Sends text or audio in their preferred language

2. Meta API

Forwards webhook POST payload securely

3. AI Engine

Processes text/voice via multilingual LLM & RAG

4. Core Systems

Retrieves user profile & database context

Core Capabilities

  • Voice Note Processing: Seamlessly transcribes multi-language audio messages (including Kinyarwanda, English, French, and Swahili), processes them with the AI engine, and responds in natural text.
  • Secure Business Grounding (RAG): Feeds company policies, catalogs, or billing rules into Qdrant vector storage, ensuring the bot never hallucinates or makes up terms.
  • System Integrations: Connects to your internal APIs to handle automated operations (e.g., checking mobile money balances, order tracking, or utility registration).
